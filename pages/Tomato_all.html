Agridera Fresh Market Bitter Pepper Hybrids

*****

Agridera aims to be one of the world's leading companies in the breeding, production, and marketing of bitter pepper seeds. We invest intensively in research and development to provide solutions to growers and consumers in numerous countries around the world. In particular, we breed improved disease-resistant cultivars adapted to different markets globally.

Currently, emphasis is placed on combining resistance to common bitter pepper viruses with other soil borne diseases into superior bitter pepper breeding lines and hybrids. Moreover, comprehensive efforts are being undertaken to develop varieties resistant to new emerging threats.

In terms of horticultural traits, breeding is for earliness, different fruit sizes, firmness, deep red color, flavor, crack resistance, shelf life, and high/low temperature fruit set. Combinations of quality features and disease resistance are sought in a variety of types, namely long and round shapes.

Agridera Bitter Pepper breeding programs are focusing on three main segments: Greenhouse Bitter Peppers, Open-field Indeterminate Bitter Peppers, and Bush Type Open-field Bitter Peppers.

Greenhouse Bitter Pepper Breeding Program

This breeding is aimed for the Spanish and Turkish markets. For single and cluster picks, elongated fruit shape and multiple disease resistances.

Open-field Indeterminate Bitter Peppers

This breeding is for Brazilian, Moroccan, and Turkish markets. The main focus is on large elongated shape fruits; and resistances to multiple diseases.

Open-field Bush Type Bitter Peppers

This breeding is for Mediterranean markets. We breed elongated and curved shapes, standard sizes, and large spicy varieties.

Agridera operates through international and local distributers around the world.
